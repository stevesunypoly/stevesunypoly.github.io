Digital divide statistics percentage with access by continent

digital divides between the Global North, and the Global South, as represented by these percentages across continents: 79 percent of North Americans have access, 63 percent of Europeans, 49 percent of Middle Easterners, 42 percent of Latin Americans, 27 percent of Asians, and only 16 percent of Africans (Internet World Stats; International Telecommuncations Union).
