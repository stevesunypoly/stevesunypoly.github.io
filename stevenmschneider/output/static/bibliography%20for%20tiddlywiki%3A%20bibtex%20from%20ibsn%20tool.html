one of the things that needs to happen to make tiddlywiki a platform for all of life :) is that it handles bibliographic citations.

bibtex remains the best, as it is most flexible, and written in json (?) or standard stuff.

so, given an ibsn number: 9780262562201

it can be entered into the ibsn-to-bibtex generator: http://manas.tungare.name/software/isbn-to-bibtex/

to yield:

@book{9780262562201, Author = {Kirsten Foot and Steven M. Schneider}, Title = {Web Campaigning (Acting with Technology)}, Publisher = {The MIT Press}, Year = {2006}, ISBN = {0262562200}, URL = {http://www.amazon.com/Campaigning-Acting-Technology-Kirsten-Foot/dp/0262562200%3FSubscriptionId%3D0JYN1NVW651KCA56C102%26tag%3Dtechkie-20%26linkCode%3Dxm2%26camp%3D2025%26creative%3D165953%26creativeASIN%3D0262562200} }

this guy has cool stuff:

http://manas.tungare.name/software/ including

  • Shorter Google URLs Chrome Extension: Share shorter URLs for Google Search by removing the cruft, and retaining only the parameters that matter.
  • QRCode Chrome Extension: Shows the current URL as a QRCode for scanning and viewing on mobile devices.
