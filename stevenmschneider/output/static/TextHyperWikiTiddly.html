Digital Text Research Group Projects

Text | Hyper | Wiki | Tiddly is:

  1. A scholarly examination of hypertext and hypertextuality, including a fine-grained analysis of Tiddlywiki as a platform that enables and facilitates hypertetxual writing, hypertextual reading, and hypertextxual thinking.
  2. A primer on how to practice reading, writing and thinking hypertextually using Tiddlywiki
  3. A teaching platform to support advanced undergraduate/ graduate courses on hypertextuality, using Tiddlywiki as a reading / writing / design platform, including readings, lectures / presentations, and assignments / exercises.
  4. A mash-up built on top of http://tiddlywiki.com (as the source for all things TiddlyWiki)

* Scholarly work, written in TiddlyWiki: ** examining the origins, evolution and theory of digital text through the lens of the words Hyper, Wiki and Tiddly ** demonstrating practices and techniques through application in TiddlyWiki.
