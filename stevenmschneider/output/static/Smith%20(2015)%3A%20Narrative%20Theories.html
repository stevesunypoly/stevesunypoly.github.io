Suggests that there are to kinds of non-linear story telling (film): – cohesive models (which include disruptive, non sequential and disjointed narratives) and non cohesive (which include ergodic, branching and multi linear narratives). A non sequential is "where the sequence is shown differently to how it actually occured"; multi-linear is where multiple storylines or aspects of the same story intertwine..."
