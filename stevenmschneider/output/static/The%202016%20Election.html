IDT Thesis/Project Ideas: A running list
  • Folks interested in politics will find any number of topics related to the 2016 campaign. Designing a study that explores one or more of the following could be interesting:
    • the app environment that emerges around the election
    • digital platforms (twitter, facebook, etc),
    • actors engaging in digital practices (candidates, citizens, supporters, groups, etc.)
    • digital response to events (elections, debates, terrorist attacks, etc)
  • Ways of approaching this kind of data include
    • App Design & Development - prototype, review or document (for competitive analysis) apps targeting citizens
    • Cyberethnography (Wikipedia) of the digital electoral landscape.
    • Content analysis (Wikipedia)
      • of digital platforms like facebook, twitter, using If This, Then That DIY programming tools to report activities of political actors to google spreadsheets.
      • of digital media like web sites created by political actors (candidates, parties, citizens, etc.)
