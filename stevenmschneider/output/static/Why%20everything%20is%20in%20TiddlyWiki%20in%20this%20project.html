Thoughts

A quite reasonable question.

  • In my view, TiddlyWiki is the tool that currently comes closest to allowing individuals to author, read and engage with hypertexts that match the models proposed by Bush, Bnglebart, Nelson, Bolton and Landow.
  • In my view, tiddlywikificiation is the technique of hypertextualization in a manner consistent with the vision described by Nelson's Xanadu, and Englebart's ??.
  • So using Tiddlywiki is theoretically rich, tool rich and technique rich.
