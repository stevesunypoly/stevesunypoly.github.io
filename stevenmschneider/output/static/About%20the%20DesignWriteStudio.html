DesignWriteStudio

The DesignWriteStudio is a participatory, collaborative and open learning space focused on designing and writing interactive texts, and serves as the Web home for the SUNYPoly courses IDT 575 and COM 375 as well as an open version of the same course.

We use the TiddlyWiki platform to explore the practices and techniques of hypertext and hypertextuality.

The DesignWriteStudio TiddlyWiki serves as the web presence of the Studio, and as a demonstration of TiddlyWiki.
